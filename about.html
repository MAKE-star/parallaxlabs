About the studio

Why parallax

Close one eye, then the other — the world shifts slightly. That shift is parallax, and it's how depth perception works: two viewpoints, resolved into one accurate read of distance. We named the studio after it because that's exactly how we work.

One eye on engineering

Mayopo reads a build for correctness: does the ledger balance, does the lock hold under concurrency, does the webhook verify what it claims to verify.

One eye on product & data

Adekanle reads the same build for signal and shape: what's actually happening in production, where users drop off, and whether the roadmap still matches what the data says.

Neither view alone is the full picture. Together, they are.
