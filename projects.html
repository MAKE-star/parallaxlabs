Fintech engineering

Payments engine · Double-entry ledger

Payments & Ledger Engine

4.85 / 5.00 — Strong Hire

A full-stack payment processor built on Node.js/Express and raw PostgreSQL: a double-entry ledger with BIGINT minor-unit accounting, per-currency transit accounts, and point-in-time balance snapshots. A triple-layer concurrency model — Postgres advisory locks, SELECT FOR UPDATE, and SERIALIZABLE isolation — enforces deadlock-free, strictly serializable execution under stress testing. Webhooks are verified with raw-body HMAC-SHA256 and timing-safe comparison; an FX quoting service handles spread, rate-locking and TTL expiry.

Reviewer feedback described the concurrency design as "exceptionally rigorous" and the ledger schema as a "production-grade ledger model."
